About Me

I am a fresh graduate of Ateneo de Manila University under the BS Applied Physics - BS Materials Science and Engineering program. My research foci were in plasma treatment and molecular dynamics for my first and second theses projects, respectively. I have current interests in renewable energy research and the like. I have presented in two international symposia and currently working on getting my research work published.

Research Work

Seminars and Symposia Attended

My research work during my undergraduate years revolved around the treatment of surfaces using atmospheric pressure plasma as well as for Molecular Dynamics. I am one of the authors of a manual on the usage of the Large Atomic/Molecular Massively Parallel Simulator (LAMMPS) presented and used during the Vacuum Society of the Philippines' second webinar Plasma and Society II by Ms. Catherine Joy Dela Cruz. I have also presented two different studies in two international symposia. The first of these was an oral presentation in Baguio City, Philippines during the 5th International Symposium of the Vacuum Society of the Philippines where I presented my study on Molecular Dynamics. The second of these presentations was a poster presentation in Nagoya, Japan where I presented my work on Plasma Treatment of Materials at ISPlasma 2024. The links to these events are shown below.
